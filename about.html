About CustomWheel

Behind the Random Spinner Application

CustomWheel (customwheel.github.io) was born out of a simple idea: creating an online decision-making application that balances true mathematical fairness with a highly engaging, playful maximalist aesthetic. The web is full of clinical, uninspiring utility tools, so we designed CustomWheel to inject excitement, color, and absolute fun into everyday interactions.

Whether you are an educator picking students for active classroom participation, a live streamer distributing giveaway prizes to thousands of viewers, or just someone settling a friendly dinner debate, our spin wheel provides a seamless, instant celebration with confetti and smooth physics-based rendering.

Our Core Commitment

We build utilities that respect the people who use them. That means CustomWheel is completely free, contains no premium subscription locks, requires no user registrations, and processes your custom text entries completely locally within your browser context. No tracking, no data harvesting—just pure chance every single time.
