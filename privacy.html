Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 3, 2026

Welcome to CustomWheel (accessible via customwheel.github.io). Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. Because we believe in total transparency, this Privacy Policy outlines how your information is processed when you use our free digital wheel spinner application.

1. No Data Collection

Unlike many online services, CustomWheel does not collect, store, track, or share any personal data. You are not required to create an account, register, or provide an email address to use our application. All features are fully functional out of the box without any sign-up process.

2. Local Session Processing Only

Every single piece of data you input into our application—including custom wheel entries, student names, prize descriptions, or custom colors—is processed strictly locally on your own device. Your entries remain entirely within your web browser's session memory. When you close the browser tab or clear your browser data, your custom inputs are automatically cleared and never saved anywhere on our infrastructure or third-party servers.

3. Log Files & Analytics

As a static website hosted via GitHub Pages, standard web server log files may be generated automatically by the hosting provider (e.g., recording IP addresses, browser types, timestamps, and referring pages for server diagnostics and safety). CustomWheel itself does not deploy tracking analytics platforms or build individual profiles from this data.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

CustomWheel may use local browser storage configurations strictly to remember your active application preferences, such as selected color palettes or active wheel presets, for a seamless user experience. We do not use cookies for targeted behavioral advertising, cross-site tracking, or marketing activities.

5. Children's Privacy

CustomWheel is designed to be safe for all users, including teachers and students in educational settings. Because we do not harvest or store any personal data whatsoever, our service natively complies with global privacy safety standards, ensuring no data belonging to minors is ever captured or vulnerable to data leaks.

6. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this operational privacy declaration at any time. Any changes will be instantly updated directly on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions or security feedback regarding our local data protocol, please contact us by opening an issue on our public GitHub repository.
